Frequently Asked Questions

Q.

How does a doctor register for the Behavioral and Emotional Screening System?

Doctors can register by clicking on the self-registration link they receive via WhatsApp from Dr. Sandeep Kelkar and filling out the required form accurately.

Q.

What are the key features of the Behavioral and Emotional Screening System for doctors?

● The system is personalized for the doctor, which means that the patients interact with the form with the doctor’s name and clinic details. ● Doctors or clinic staff can both share screening forms with patients, and the entire system operates on WhatsApp. ● The basic screening form is available in English and Indian local languages. ● The system provides instant on-screen responses for patients, and reports are sent to doctors by email.

Q.

How can doctors send the screening form to patients?

After registration, doctors receive a link to their personalized screening system, where they can enter the patient’s WhatsApp number to send the screening form.

Q.

What languages are available for the screening form?

The basic screening form is available in English, Hindi, Marathi, Bengali, Tamil, and Telugu, whereas the advanced form is supposed to be filled up with doctor assistance and is in English.

Q.

What happens after a patient submits the screening form?

The patient receives an on-screen response highlighting any red flags and they are asked to visit you, and you will receive both a doctor’s report and a patient report via email.

Q.

How should a doctor proceed if red flags are detected in the basic screening form?

If red flags are noticed, it’s recommended to prescribe advanced screening for further evaluation.

Q.

How should a doctor proceed if concerns are identified in the advanced screening report?

The generated reports will advise you on further course of action and give you access to EQ Specialists for a referral/consultation.

Q.

How is the doctor compensated for the time spent in helping the patient to fill up the advanced screening form?

For every advanced screening product consultation, doctors receive 250 rs as their fee for advising the patient for filling up the form. This is a part of the Rs 499 collected by the system operator from the patient. Your fee is paid to you by the system operator on a fortnightly/monthly basis.

Q.

Can the system be used for all age groups?

Yes, the system has 4 advanced screening forms for the age groups 0-3 years, 3-6 years, 7-11 years and 12-17 years.

Q.

What technical requirements are needed to use the system?

A mobile phone with WhatsApp and Gmail.

Q.

How secure is patient data within the system?

Patient identifiable information is not stored by the system for the basic screening form in the case of advanced screening forms where the patient has made the payment the information is stored in a secure manner.

Q.

Are there training materials available for using the system?

The system does not require any training because it works on WhatsApp and Gmail and there is no app to download. To know how the system works click here to download the PDF document.

Q.

What should a doctor do if they encounter technical issues with the system?

Doctors should contact the support team by messaging by WhatsApp on +91-8297634553 for any technical issues faced. The system operator support team will get in touch with you within 24 hours.

Q.

How does the system integrate with existing patient management systems?

Any reports sent to you can be added by you to any patient management system that you currently use in a manner similar to the addition of lab diagnostic reports.

Q.

Who is the Service Provider?

The Emoscreen solution has been designed by Dr. Sandeep Kelkar and his team at Equipoise Learning Private Limited. The system operator is Inditech Technology Services Private Limited (The same company that operates the dIAP system of IAP) and the payment partner is Razorpay.
